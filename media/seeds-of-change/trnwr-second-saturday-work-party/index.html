TRNWR Second Saturday Work Party

TRNWR’s Second Saturday Work Party to help remove invasive Blackberry bushes.

Date not listed on original post

By Next-Gen Innovators

On July 12th, Tualatin River National Wildlife Refuge(TRNWR) held their monthly second Saturday work party. Team member Andrew participated in intensive work, helping with the removal of invasive species like Blackberry bushes along with other volunteers.
