The team behind NGI

Students, founders, and alumni working toward youth-led environmental change.

Current team

Leading Next-Gen Innovators

Andrew Ma

Executive Officer

Andrew Ma is a sophomore at Jesuit High School. Passionate about climate change, he hopes to bring together and inspire the younger generation to help solve environmental challenges. In doing so, he wishes to build a strong community committed to sustainable change. He wants NGI to provide this community the resources necessary to make a serious impact on various environmental issues. In his free time, he enjoys playing tennis, hiking, and painting landscapes.
