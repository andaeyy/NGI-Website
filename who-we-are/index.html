Who We Are

Founded in 2020, Next-Gen Innovators is a student-led volunteer organization committed to creating a launchpad for youth environmental change via a network of action-minded resources in order to make impact-creating innovation a reality in our fight for the environment.

Our mission is to lead lasting positive impact, connectivity, and growth among the youth community by serving as a launchpad for the ideas of changemakers.

What we do Meet our team

Sustainability

Sustainability

Our mission as stewards of the environment is based on the ethical objectives of the United Nations Sustainable Development Goals. Through our work we hope to lower and eventually eradicate the impacts of global warming on earth. We also aim to lower the upcoming generation's ecological footprint by teaching the youth through guest speakers and our knowledge how to live a more green life. Through the initiative of NGI and other youth encouraging climate-focused companies we want to create a generation that will take initiative, and use their knowledge to create a better world.

Impact

Impact

Since 2020, we've set off in the hopes of encouraging each coming generation to continue the fight against climate change. Over the years since, we've organized hackathons, cleanups, youth summits, multi-generational conversations, and more to help support our fight for a cleaner world.

Our growing community of innovators is constantly on the rise, so feel free to join us at any time!
