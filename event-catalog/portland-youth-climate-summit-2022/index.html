'22 Portland Youth Climate Summit

The Portland Youth Climate Collective presented a youth-led summit with presentations on environmental justice, intersectional environmentalism, Indigenous-led environmentalism, food justice, and urban development.

Date: April 23, 2022

Time: 10 AM-4:30 PM

Location: OMSI

Event link

The Portland Youth Climate Collective presented the 2nd Annual Portland Youth Climate Summit at OMSI. This youth-led climate summit included presentations on issues such as Environmental Justice, Intersectional Environmentalism, Indigenous Led Environmentalism, Food Justice, and Urban Development.
