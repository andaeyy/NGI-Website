Vision and Mission

Vision

Channeling the inspiring voices of youth and serving as a launchpad for action, empowering generations of youth to take the initiative and solve the World's climate crisis.

Mission

Leading the wave of change and growth among the youth community by serving as the root to both teach and encourage the next generation
